Issues Men Face

  • Bias in dowry and harassment laws.
  • Lack of adoption rights for single men.
  • No exclusive schemes or financial support.
  • Society ignores men’s struggles and mental health.

Equality Agenda

We believe laws should be gender-neutral and based on character, not assumptions. Justice should treat criminals as criminals, not men vs women. Real equality must work both ways.
